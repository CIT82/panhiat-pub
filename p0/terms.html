Terms of Service

  • The services we provide


    At Little Learner Academy, we offer both online and onsite teaching programs designed to cater to diverse learning needs. Our online platform provides 24/7 access to courses, allowing students to learn at their own pace, anytime, anywhere. For families who prefer in-person education, we also offer onsite classes with experienced teachers. Our curriculum is designed around core subjects like English, math, and writing, ensuring that every child builds a strong academic foundation. Operating like a charter school, we prioritize personalized learning experiences that promote both academic excellence and personal growth.

  • How our services are funded


    Little Learner Academy is committed to providing quality education at an affordable cost. Our services are funded through a combination of tuition fees, grants, and donations from our generous community of supporters. This blended funding model allows us to maintain state-of-the-art facilities, hire the best teachers, and provide access to essential educational resources. We also offer scholarships and financial aid to eligible families to ensure that every child has access to quality education, regardless of their financial situation.

  • Your commitments to Little Learners and our community


    When you enroll your child at Little Learner Academy, you become a part of a supportive and engaged learning community. We ask families to commit to regular communication with teachers, participate in parent-teacher conferences, and support their child's educational journey by fostering a learning environment at home. Additionally, we encourage families to be involved in school events and contribute to the growth of our academy by offering feedback, volunteering, or participating in community outreach activities. Together, we build a nurturing and inclusive educational space where children can thrive.

  • Additional Provisions


    To ensure a smooth and productive online learning experience, students are required to have access to a stable internet connection, a computer or tablet, and the necessary software for coursework. Parents or guardians may need to assist younger children with technology setup and provide a quiet, distraction-free space for learning. Our academy provides technical support and troubleshooting assistance to ensure families can fully engage in the online learning process. We also offer live tutoring sessions and interactive virtual classrooms, ensuring that students remain connected to their teachers and peers.

  • Other terms and policies that may apply to you


    All purchases are subject to our refund and cancellation policies, which are designed to be fair and flexible. By contributing through donations or course purchases, families help us continue our mission of providing exceptional education to all students.

    1. Refund Policy: Our refund policy allows families to request a full or partial refund within 14 days of enrollment, depending on the course package selected.

    2. Enrollment Deadlines: Enrollment deadlines are set at the start of each academic term, with late enrollments accepted on a case-by-case basis, subject to availability.

    3. Technology Usage Agreement: All students and families are required to adhere to our technology usage agreement, which outlines the responsible use of devices and internet access for online learning to ensure a safe and productive educational environment.
