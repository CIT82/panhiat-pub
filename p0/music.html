Music

At Little Learners Academy, we believe that music is a universal language that enriches lives and fosters creativity, confidence, and self-expression. Our music classes offer students of all ages the opportunity to explore a wide range of musical instruments, develop their talents, and build a lifelong love for music.

Physical Activities

Guitar

The guitar is a versatile string instrument that comes in various styles, including acoustic and electric. It is popular in many music genres, from rock to classical, and is known for its rich sound and ability to accompany singing.

Piano

The piano is a large keyboard instrument known for its expressive range. It has 88 keys and is used in a wide variety of music styles. Pianos can be acoustic or digital and are essential in both solo and ensemble performances.

Violin

The violin is a string instrument played with a bow. It is known for its beautiful tone and is a key instrument in orchestras and chamber music. The violin has a range of over four octaves and can produce both melodic and harmonic sounds.

Drums

Drums are percussion instruments that provide the rhythm in many musical styles. They can range from simple hand drums to complex drum kits, which include various drums and cymbals, allowing for a wide range of sounds and dynamics.

Flute

The flute is a woodwind instrument that produces sound by blowing air across an opening. It is known for its sweet and light tone, often used in classical and contemporary music. Flutes come in various types, including the concert flute and piccolo.

Trumpet

The trumpet is a brass instrument known for its powerful and bright sound. It is commonly used in orchestras, jazz bands, and brass ensembles. With three valves, the trumpet can play a wide range of notes and is a favorite among many musicians.
