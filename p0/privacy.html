Privacy Policy

  • 1. Information We Collect

    We may collect personal information when you:

    • Register for our school or online programs
    • Make payments for tuition or courses
    • Contact us for support or inquiries
    • Access our online learning platform

    The types of information we collect include:

    • Personal Details: Names, addresses, email addresses, phone numbers, and date of birth
    • Educational Records: Student progress, test scores, and teacher feedback
    • Payment Information: Credit card details, billing address, and payment history
    • Technical Information: IP addresses, browser type, device information, and usage data for online platforms

  • 2. How We Use Your Information

    We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

    • To provide and manage educational services, including online and onsite programs
    • To communicate with you regarding your child’s progress, school events, and updates
    • To process payments and issue receipts
    • To improve our services by analyzing usage data and feedback
    • To ensure the safety and security of students, both online and onsite

  • 3. How We Share Your Information

    Little Learner School will not sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information under the following circumstances:

    • With service providers, such as payment processors or IT support, who assist in delivering our services
    • With educational platforms used for online learning and assessments
    • When required by law or government authorities, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of the school, students, or others
    • With your consent, in specific situations such as sharing information with external educators or organizations for extracurricular activities

  • 4. Data Security

    We take data security seriously and have implemented appropriate measures to protect your information. This includes encryption of sensitive data, secure payment gateways, and restricted access to student records. While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

  • 5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

    Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to improve user experience, track usage, and enhance site performance. Cookies are small files placed on your device to collect information about your browsing habits. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings; however, disabling cookies may limit your ability to use some features of our site.

  • 6. Children’s Privacy

    We are committed to protecting the privacy of children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent. If we learn that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will take steps to delete it as soon as possible. Parents and guardians have the right to review, request deletion, or withdraw consent for the collection of their child’s personal information at any time.

  • 7. Your Rights

    You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

    • Access: You may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you or your child.
    • Correction: You can request corrections to any inaccurate or incomplete information.

    Our website and online platforms may contain links to third-party websites or services. Little Learner School is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any site you visit.

  • 9. Updates to Our Privacy Policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on our website with the revised date. We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
